<objective>
Switch all 4 Railway healthcheck paths from /q/health/ready (DB-dependent) to /q/health/live
(JVM-only) so that transient DB unavailability during cold starts or redeployments no longer
causes Railway to mark services unhealthy and return 502 to all traffic.

Update test_integration.sh to check both /q/health/live (liveness) and /q/health/ready
(readiness) per service so operators can distinguish "JVM dead" from "DB connectivity issue".

Purpose: Eliminate false-positive service kills where the JVM is healthy but DB is momentarily
unreachable. This is the primary root cause of the current prod 502 incidents.

Output: 4 updated railway.toml files, 1 updated test_integration.sh.
</objective>
